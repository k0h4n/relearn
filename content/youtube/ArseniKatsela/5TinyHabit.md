Summary from video of Arseni Katsela with titled [5 Tiny Habits to INSANE Art Progress](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pBxerxHZsEU)

- ## Input Detox
	- There 2 type of content:
		- Deep Content
		- Shallow Content
	- Block shallow stuff
	- Focus on true learning
- ## The Anti Practice
	- The key is find something boredom without your phone
- ## The Permadeath Timer
	- Set a timer for 10 min session and if there is a tiny distraction you have to start all over again
- ## Auto-Solve Switch
	- The Rule: When you are stuck, you have to physically leave the room and do anything mindless
- ## Source Code Method
	- Look for the sketch and not the final result
	- Try to copy the sketch construction line and gesture and try to come out with the detail on your own
