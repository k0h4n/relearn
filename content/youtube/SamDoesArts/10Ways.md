Summary from video of SamDoesArts with titled [10 EASY WAYS to Improve Your Art In 2026 (beginner friendly)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U3Ajr2cLd4k)

- ## Draw what scares you
  - Draw something you always avoiding (your weakness)
- ## Fix your form
  - Draw with proper hand grip (proper form) to reduce fatigue or injury later on
- ## Accept your imperfection
  - Accept your drawing imperfection, you don't have to post it. Just keep drawing and move on
- ## Finish your drawing
  - Be responsible and finish your drawing
	- It will make you discipline
	- Just set the deadline and finish it on the timeframe
	- Move on if you can't finish it
- ## Draw More
  - Drawing is like gacha with increase probability you can make it good every time you draw
	- Don't settle with shortcut or hack
- ## Keep a sketch book
  - Sketchbook provide a space that encourage mistake
	- Use it to have fun
- ## Set Goals
  - Make sure you have control over the goals (you cannot control to get 100 likes in instagram)
	- Make it quantifiable (like draw anatomy for 10 minutes everyday)
	- Another example is like i want to make 3 finish pieces every month
- ## Study fundamentals
  - Know the rules before you break them
	- List of fundamental rules:
		- Anatomy
		- Value
		- Color
		- Perspective
- ## Use References
  - The more you use refence the more mental library you will make
- ## Stop Listening to Everyone
  - Listen to the the expert, some people like to share their skill but sometimes it will hurt your art journey habit
	- Consume less and create more
		- If you consume more than you create, then there is something wrong with your system
		- Time is your most precious resource
